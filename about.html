About the Artist

James Annable is an acrylic painter who works out of his home studio in Vancouver, BC.

He has received instruction at Emily Carr University as well as several independent studios within Vancouver. He enjoys creating still life paintings that reconsider familiar items using unexpected compositions and repetition.
